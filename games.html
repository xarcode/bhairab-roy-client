Game Walkthrough

Join the cat-tastic adventure in this endless platformer game! Run, jump, and dodge your way through a colorful world full of enemy foxes and sparkling diamonds. How far can you go before you run out of lives? Press spacebar to jump over obstacles and gaps, and collect as many diamonds as you can to increase your score. Be careful: the foxes will try to stop you, and the speed will get faster as you progress. This game is easy to play but hard to master. Can you beat your own high score and become the ultimate cat hero?

×

Game Rules

  • 1. The game starts with the cat running on a scrolling landscape. The landscape will have gaps, platforms, foxes, and diamonds.
  • 2. The cat has 5 health points (hp) at the beginning of the game. The hp is shown at the top left corner of the screen.
  • 3. The player can press spacebar to make the cat jump. The cat can jump over gaps, platforms, and foxes. The cat cannot jump over the edge of the screen or fall into a gap.
  • 4. The player can collect diamonds by touching them with the cat. Each diamond is worth 10 points. The score is shown at the top right corner of the screen.
  • 5. The player can avoid foxes by jumping over them or running past them. If the cat touches a fox, it will lose 1 hp and flash briefly. If the cat loses all its hp, the game is over.

Game Rules

  • 1. The game starts with the hero running on a platform. The platforms are randomly generated and have different lengths and heights.
